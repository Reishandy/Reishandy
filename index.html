General Disclosures

Comprehensive Legal Policy

This section provides standard legal disclosures regarding data handling, intended for regulatory compliance.

1. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using my services, I do not require you to provide any personally identifiable information unless strictly necessary for core functionality. Any information that is requested will be retained locally on your device and is not collected by me in any way.

2. Log Data

Whenever you use my applications, in a case of an error, the app may collect data and information (through native OS crash reporters) on your phone called Log Data. This may include your device IP address, device name, operating system version, and crash timestamps. This is exclusively used for debugging and is never tied to your personal identity.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

My services do not use "cookies" to track behavior across the web. Any local storage utilized is solely to save your local application states and preferences.

4. Service Providers

I do not employ third-party companies or individuals to facilitate my services or perform service-related analytics. Your data footprint remains directly between you and the application.

5. Security

Because my applications prioritize local processing, the risk of data exposure is minimized by design. I deploy standard cryptographic practices to ensure that any local data you store remains strictly within your control.

6. Children's Privacy

These services do not address anyone under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Application Specifics

Huex

The following clauses apply specifically to this application and supersede the general policy where noted.

Huex is a color palette extraction and gallery organization tool. It is designed to function entirely on-device with maximum respect for your personal photos.

Photo Library Access

Huex requests access to your Photo Library to analyze images, extract dominant color data, and categorize your photos by hue. This processing happens entirely locally on your device. Your photos, image metadata, and extracted color palettes are never uploaded to the cloud, sent to remote servers, or shared with third parties.

Local Storage & Processing

Any categorization or metadata generated by the app (such as grouped color buckets or extracted palettes) is saved locally on your device's internal storage. This is done solely to improve performance during your next session and remains entirely under your control.

Your Beacon Simulator

The following clauses apply specifically to this application and supersede the general policy where noted.

Your Beacon Simulator is a developer utility designed to assist with testing and prototyping proximity-based features. It requires specific permissions to operate.

Bluetooth & Location Access

This app requests Bluetooth and Location access solely to enable the broadcast (transmission) and discovery (ranging) of iBeacons. These permissions are strictly required by the iOS operating system to interface with the hardware necessary to simulate iBeacon signals for your testing environment.

Local Processing

Your precise location data (GPS coordinates) is never tracked, stored, or transmitted to any server. The app only listens for the presence of specific, predefined Bluetooth iBeacons. No continuous location history is maintained. All simulation data is transient and exists only while the application is active.
